About Me

I am Proshat Javid, a dedicated Breath Healing therapist passionate about guiding individuals towards inner peace and well-being through conscious breathing techniques. My journey began with a personal discovery of the transformative power of breath, leading me to deeply explore ancient practices and modern therapeutic approaches.

My philosophy centers on the profound connection between breath and life. By mastering the breath, we unlock our body's natural ability to heal, reduce stress, and enhance mental clarity. I believe in a holistic approach, integrating mind, body, and spirit to foster a sustainable sense of calm and vitality.
